ccLab Project A: Creature Profile

1. Scientific Name of the Creature:

Lord of the Flies

2. Discovered by (Scientist’s Name) / at (Time of Discovery):

Discovered by ??? in 1941 (too old, can’t find information)

3. Family:

Haven’t seen any similar creatures yet. Somehow it seems like a leader of a bunch of other kinds of insects because they are fetching food only for the creature itself

4. Habitat:

Under urban flyovers, in the polluted vegetation composed of vines and mosses.

5. Appearance:

It looks like a very ordinary caterpillar at the beginning of its life. Rapidly grows into a huge moth with only one mysterious eye. Then it would be eaten by its own wings and form a cocoon to be reborn.

6. Key Characteristics:

It has a strong will to gain unlimited strength in its limited life. Somehow it seems like it is the strength that makes its life short.

7. What do we know so far about the creature?

It has a very mysterious mechanism of being infinitely reborn. Probably have the ability to control other creatures’ minds because the scientists would automatically feed the creatures without noticing their own actions.

8. How do we interact with this creature?

Feed it. Make it satisfied. Never disobey.
